Accommodation

Hello HydroXplorers!

Great news from San Carlos, HydroXpeditions is now also offering accommodation in this wonderful finca located at the foot of "la Piedra del Tabor" the most famous hiking spot of San Carlos and the tallest rock formation in Antioquia! You want to rest before or after an Xpedition? Or are you looking for a base before your hike to the top of "La Piedra"? We will be more than happy to accommodate you. The finca has 3 rooms with a total of 6 beds, a natural swimming pool with water coming from a nearby source, a small and private natural pool and some amazing views of the surrounding mountains.
